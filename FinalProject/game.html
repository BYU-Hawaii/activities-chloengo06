Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a classic two-player game where players take turns marking spaces in an 8x8 grid. One player uses "X," and the other uses "O." The goal is to be the first to place four of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row. Players alternate turns, strategically placing their marks to either complete their row or block their opponent from completing theirs. It's a fun and engaging game that combines strategy and quick thinking, making it enjoyable for players of all ages.

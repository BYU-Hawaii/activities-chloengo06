Natural Beauty

Home to terraced rice fields, lush valleys, and ethnic minority communities, Sapa offers breathtaking trekking opportunities. Visitors can explore local villages, learn about traditional customs, and experience the natural beauty of the mountains.

Cultural Heritage

The capital city, Hanoi, is known for its centuries-old architecture and a rich cultural history. Key attractions include the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, the Temple of Literature, and the bustling streets of the Old Quarter where traditional crafts and modern cafes coexist.

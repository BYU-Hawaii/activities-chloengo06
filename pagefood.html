Pho

Pho is a traditional Vietnamese noodle soup consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat, usually beef or chicken. It is a popular street food in Vietnam and is often enjoyed as a hearty breakfast or comforting meal any time of the day.

Bun Bo Hue

Bun Bo Hue is a spicy beef noodle soup that originated in the city of Hue in central Vietnam. It features thick rice noodles, tender slices of beef, pork hock, and a flavorful broth seasoned with lemongrass, chili, and shrimp paste.

Banh Mi

Banh Mi is a Vietnamese sandwich made with a French baguette and filled with a variety of ingredients such as grilled pork, pate, pickled vegetables, fresh herbs, and chili sauce. It is a delicious fusion of Vietnamese and French culinary influences.

Bun Cha

Bun Cha is a Vietnamese dish featuring grilled pork patties served with vermicelli noodles, fresh herbs, and a dipping sauce. It is typically served with a side of crispy spring rolls and is a favorite among locals and visitors alike.
